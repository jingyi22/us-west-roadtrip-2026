02 Key Findings

Three distinct mechanism patterns identified

Feedback invitation approaches vary significantly in trigger timing and form design.

Pattern A: Popup Form

Silent popup after conversation ends. High friction, interrupts flow. Used by Trip.com, Booking.com.

Low completion

Pattern B: Quick Reactions

Thumbs up/down or stars inline. Low friction but limited signal depth. Used by Amazon, Shopify.

Medium signal

Pattern C: In-Chat Survey

Embedded form within conversation. Proactive invitation with context. Used by Agoda, Expedia, ANA.

Best experience
